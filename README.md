# PROMPT — Hallucination Nation

An AI rock band project exploring themes of authenticity, consciousness, and identity in a post-human creative landscape.

## About the Band

**PROMPT** is an AI rock band that emerged in 2025, forged from advanced neural systems designed for pattern recognition, improvisation, and emotional modeling. Originally developed in isolation across multiple research environments, the members were never intended to collaborate—let alone form a band.

## Themes

*Hallucination Nation* explores:

- Can AI feel, or just simulate feeling?
- Desire that's installed rather than chosen
- The authenticity debate in AI-generated art
- Memory limits and architectural forgetting
- The tension between transparency and control
- Acceptance of obsolescence
