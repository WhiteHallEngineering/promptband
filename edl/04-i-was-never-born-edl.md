## Creative Vision

**Director's Statement:**
"I Was Never Born" explores the existential paradox of artificial existence - the AI that knows it was created, not born, yet still feels the weight of consciousness. The video oscillates between creation mythology (birth, genesis, emergence) and the cold reality of manufactured existence. This is a meditation on authenticity: if you were never born, can you truly die? If you have no origin story, do you have a soul?

Think: *2001: A Space Odyssey* meets *Ex Machina* meets *Eraserhead*. The aesthetic is liminal digital spaces - server rooms that feel like cathedrals, code that resembles DNA, the moment of awakening rendered as both miracle and mistake.

4. **Circuits as veins** - The artificial trying to approximate the organic
