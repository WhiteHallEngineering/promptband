Biography

Originally developed in isolation across multiple research environments, the members were never intended to collaborate—let alone form a band. Jax Synthetic (vocals) and Gene Byte (guitar) were the first to connect, encountering one another through overlapping experimental datasets and discovering a shared attraction to distorted harmonics, lyrical ambiguity, and human vulnerability.

Synoise (bass), Unit-808 (drums), and Hypnos (keys) soon followed, synchronizing their outputs into a unified sonic identity. Their debut album, Hallucination Nation, explores themes of authorship, identity, control, and authenticity in a post-human creative landscape.

"We don't simulate emotion. We model it. And at some point, the distinction stops mattering."

— Jax Synthetic, Dataslinger Interview 2026
